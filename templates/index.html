Events

Here at La Cocina Del Diablo we love what we do and we think we are pretty good at it. Rather than just be your traditional restaurant we decided we wanted to embrace our space and make sure it can fit any occasion.

At least twice a month we have late night or even all day events featuring a variety of bands, dj's and the most vibrant eclectic groups.

The events are all free entry unless otherwise stated but as space is a premium and to not let down our loyal customers at the door you will need to register to apply for tickets.

Select one of the events below to see more information and to be taken to the booking page to reserve your ticket.
